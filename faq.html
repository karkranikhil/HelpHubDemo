FAQs

Find answers to the most common questions about our services and products.

How do I create a new Salesforce account?

To create a Salesforce account, visit the Salesforce login page and click "Sign Up." Fill in your details, including your email, company name, and role.

Once registered, you will receive a verification email. Click the link to activate your account and gain access to Salesforce features.

How can I reset my Salesforce password?

If you forget your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email to receive a reset link.

Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password. Use a strong, unique password for security.

How do I set up Salesforce Lightning?

Salesforce Lightning is enabled from Setup → User Interface → Lightning Experience. Ensure your profile has Lightning access.

After enabling, you can switch to Lightning from the user menu. Explore features like App Builder and enhanced dashboards.

How do I create reports and dashboards?

Navigate to the Reports tab, click "New Report," select the object, and configure filters and fields. Save the report.

For dashboards, use the Dashboard tab to add components based on your reports. Drag and drop charts to visualize data effectively.

How can I integrate Salesforce with external apps?

Salesforce offers AppExchange for pre-built integrations or you can use REST/SOAP APIs for custom integrations.

Ensure API permissions are enabled in Setup. Use authentication keys to connect securely with external systems.

What is Salesforce Flow and how do I use it?

Salesforce Flow allows automation of business processes using declarative tools. You can create flows for approvals, notifications, or data updates.

Access Flow Builder from Setup → Process Automation → Flows. Use triggers, elements, and logic to design your automated process.

How do I manage Salesforce user permissions?

User permissions are managed via Profiles and Permission Sets. Profiles control default permissions, while Permission Sets grant additional rights.

Assign Permission Sets carefully to avoid conflicts and ensure users have access to the necessary objects and fields.

How can I import data into Salesforce?

Use Data Import Wizard for small imports or Data Loader for large datasets. Map CSV fields to Salesforce objects and validate data before import.

Always perform imports in a sandbox first to avoid data issues in production.

How do I track Salesforce records?

Use standard reports, dashboards, and list views to track record progress. Set up notifications or scheduled reports for timely updates.

Customize reports with filters, groupings, and charts to monitor key metrics and KPIs efficiently.

How do I get Salesforce support?

Salesforce support is available through the Help Portal or by submitting a case from within Salesforce.

Provide detailed information about your issue, including object names, screenshots, and error messages, for faster resolution.
