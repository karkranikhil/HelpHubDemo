Policies

Understand our policies to ensure a smooth experience.

Salesforce Data Privacy Policy

We respect user data and follow strict Salesforce data handling standards. All customer data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Access to data is restricted based on user roles and permissions. Employees undergo compliance training to ensure privacy.

We adhere to GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant regulations to maintain data integrity and user trust.

Salesforce Usage Policy

Users must not share login credentials and must follow best practices for security. Use Salesforce only for authorized business purposes.

Monitor your usage to avoid exceeding API limits or storage quotas. Report any suspicious activity to the admin team immediately.

Misuse of Salesforce resources may result in account suspension or termination.

Salesforce Refund & Service Policy

Salesforce subscriptions are billed monthly or annually. Refunds are processed according to Salesforce terms if services are canceled within a defined period.

Support is available for all subscription tiers. Users can request service assistance via the Salesforce Help Portal or through internal support channels.

We strive to maintain service uptime and ensure customer satisfaction according to agreed SLAs.
